Brief Introduction

Veggies is a casual puzzle game with the core of selling vegetables. I developed it during the COVID-19 pandemic last year. The main idea is to create a relaxing world to help people reduce anxiety. In the game, the goal of players is to put vegetables to the place where customers can easily get and buy. The players will make profits when they successfully sell the vegetables. The ultimate goal is to meet the requirement of the target income.

Background

There is a beautiful village called "Vege Village", where a girl called Lily grows up. She is an orphan, but fortunately, under the care and love of the villagers, she has gradually grown into a lively and lovely girl. She runs in the fields every day, enjoying the smell of sunlight on vegetables. However, wonderful days do not last long. Due to the rapid development of the city, a lot of lands are expropriated. People tend to migrate to city for employment, leading to decreasing population. Life becomes difficult for everyone who is still in the village. Lily will never allow her beloved Vege Village to disappear. She needs to earn money to repay the people who helped her. So she opens a vegetable shop in the town, and people like the sweet and juicy vegetables growed in the Vege Village. Thanks to Lily's efforts, more and more people begin to call for the preservation of the rural scenery of the Vegetable Village.

High Concept Statement

When you first see this game, you may think that it is a match-3 game. Match-3 games are very popular in the market due to their simplicity and easy-to-handle characteristics, but there are not many variants of match-3 games. So I hope to retain the easy-to-handle characteristics of match-3 games in this game, and allow players to use their wisdom and strategy to achieve a greater sense of accomplishment from the exploration of playing method.

Numerical Mechanics

Players can try to eliminate multiple columns at the same time. Multiple columns elimination takes up more time so that it is a high-risk activity. I designed a high return for achieving multiple columns elimination to reward this kind of risk. Lots of test and adjustment were done by me and the figure on the right shows the final difficulty and yield data when multiple columns were eliminated simultaneously. As you can see, the final difficulty and yield are almost proportional. Therefore, players can choose their own strategy freely without worry about return.

Combined with friendly score levels, both single-column elimination preferred players and multi-columns elimination preferred players can complete their goal successfully.

Art Style

Vision

The game used 2D hand-painted style in general, with fresh and bright colors to convey a peaceful and refreshing keynote. The vegetables are designed to be red, orange, bright green and other warm colors to show it's sweet and delicious. A large area in the center is left white to make it easy for players to recognize and focus on the main elements.

Audio

I chose and bought this light music to convey a idea of relaxing in my game. Its main melody uses a lot of lovely whistling sound, just like the relaxing life in a small town.
